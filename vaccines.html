Pfizer-BioNTech

Ages Recommended: 5+ years old
Primary Dose(s): 2, given 3 weeks apart
Booster Dose: Everyone ages 18 years and older should get a booster dose of Pfizer-BioNTech (COVID-19 vaccines) 5 months after the last dose in their primary series.
When Fully vaccinated: 2 weeks after 2nd dose

Moderna

Ages Recommended: 18+ years old
Primary Dose(s): 2, given 4 weeks apart
Booster Dose: Everyone ages 18 years and older should get a booster dose of Moderna (COVID-19 vaccines) 5 months after the last dose in their primary series.
When Fully vaccinated: 2 weeks after 2nd dose

Johnson & Johnson

Ages Recommended: 5+ years old
Primary Dose(s): 1 dose
Booster Dose: Everyone ages 18 years and older should get a booster dose of either Pfizer-BioNTech or Moderna (COVID-19 vaccines) at least 2 months after the first dose of J&J COVID-19 vaccine.
When Fully vaccinated: 2 weeks after 1st dose
